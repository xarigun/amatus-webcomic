# AMATUS

**Amatus** is a psychological dark romance webcomic by **Xarigun**.

Set in Los Angeles during the late 1980s, the story follows **Lucas**, a refined serial killer, and **Aiden**, a mysterious young man whose presence changes the course of his life. Their bond evolves into a dangerous and intimate relationship shaped by obsession, duality, violence, and unconditional love.

## Main characters

### Lucas
A wealthy, intelligent, controlled serial killer with a philosophical inner voice and an obsessive search for meaning, beauty, and emotional truth.

### Aiden
A mysterious and emotionally complex young man whose presence disrupts Lucas's inner order and awakens a deeper form of love.

### Adam
A destructive and instinctive second personality connected to Aiden, representing chaos, danger, and unfiltered desire.
