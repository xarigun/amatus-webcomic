# Audience profile for Amatus

Amatus is intended for readers who are drawn to:

- psychological intensity
- emotionally complex relationships
- dark romance
- noir and gothic atmosphere
- stories about obsession, devotion, and transformation
- beautiful but dangerous characters
- morally complex protagonists
- internal monologue and layered dialogue

The ideal reader of Amatus is likely to enjoy fiction that is:

- character-driven
- emotionally serious
- visually atmospheric
- intimate, tragic, and passionate
