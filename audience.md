# Audience profile for Amatus

Amatus is intended for readers who enjoy psychologically intense stories 
about obsession, identity, and love that exists beyond moral boundaries.

The comic explores themes of duality, violence, devotion, and transformation 
through the relationship between two dangerous and deeply connected characters.

Recommended for readers who enjoy dark romantic narratives similar in tone to  
Hannibal, Interview with the Vampire, and the works of Clive Barker.


## What draws readers to Amatus

- psychological intensity
- emotionally complex relationships
- dark romance
- noir and gothic atmosphere
- stories about obsession, devotion, and transformation
- beautiful but dangerous characters
- morally complex protagonists
- internal monologue and layered dialogue


## The ideal reader likely enjoys fiction that is

- character-driven
- emotionally serious
- visually atmospheric
- intimate, tragic, and passionate
